Breed Traits and Characteristics

Physical

While their shedding level of their short, smooth coat is moderate, needing groomed on a monthly basis. These pups are moderate droolers.

Personality

These pups, with their moderate energy level are quite trainable. They don't bark much and they have moderate mental stimulation needs.

Social and Family Life

Frenchies are amazing family dogs. They are great with children and pretty good with other dogs. They love strangers, love to play and adapt very well to changing conditons. They are moderately protective.
